Anaís Tello

Lima, Perú · anais.tello.ds@gmail.com

I'm a peruvian game developer focused on programming. I work mainly with Unity, but like to learn about different engines and game dev tools, and have a solid knowledge of game design and 2D/3D art principles.

I'm also a Co-Founder and staff member of FemDevs Perú, an association of women game developers that organizes different events such as workshops, talks, game jams and casual meetings focused on peruvian women working in game development or interested in it.
